WHAT'S THE PROBLEM?

Local information is everywhere, but rarely in one place, easy to search, or available in the user's own language.

Local information is everywhere — but rarely simple to access. Answers can take too much time.

  • Information is scattered across websites, PDFs, directories, maps and social posts
  • Search results are often outdated, incomplete, or hard to navigate
  • Language barriers make local knowledge harder for visitors to use
  • Traditional chatbots still rely on typing, menus and static responses

People should not have to search five different places to answer one simple question. Whether they are arriving in a new town, planning their day, or looking for a trusted local business, they need fast, useful answers in a format that feels natural.
